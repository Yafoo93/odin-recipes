FUFU

GHANAIAN FUFU DESCRIBTION

This is Ghanaian native food called fufu, it is made from fresh casava,
the cassava is peeled whilst fresh and boiled to soften
it is then pounded until it beomes soft and forms together
Fufu is best served with palm nut soap, light soup, groundnut soup
of green green soup

The Fufu Ingredient

  • cassava
  • Water

SOUP INGREDIENT

  • Palm Nut
  • Water
  • Fish or meat
  • pepper
  • tomatoes
  • pepper
  • salt

STEPS FOR PREPARATION

  1. peel the fresh casava and boil till it softens
  2. Pound the boiled cassava till it forms
  3. cook the palm nut till it softens
  4. Pound the palm nuts to remove palm kernel
  5. sieve the pounded palm nuts and and put it on fire
  6. add the neccessary Ingredients to make the soap tasty
